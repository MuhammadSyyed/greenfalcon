Green Falcon is a leading distributor of chemicals and ingredients in the MENA region. Based in Saudi Arabia, the company specialized in managing complex supply chains for both chemical manufacturers and consumers, simplifying market access to thousands of products and services. Combining a global network with exceptional local execution, Green Falcon stands as on the industry's most effective and preferred chennels to the MENA market for partners.

YOUR TRUSTED PARTNER FOR CASE AND CONSTRUCTION

At Green Falcon, we are committed to delivering innovative, high-quality solutions for the CASE and Construction industry sectors. With our global reach, local expertise, and extensive portfolio of specialty products, we empower manufacturers to achieve exceptional performance and tailored formulations. Our wide range of ever evolving comprehensive portfolio includes it all. From Binders to Additives. Our solutions are designed to meet the evolving demands of the industry, ensuring efficiency, sustainability, and cost-effectiveness.
